Formula

Trip time:
The trip time is calculated as the total distance divided by the average speed.
In this case, 336.5 km / 110 km/hr = 3.05 hours (approx. 3 hours).

Current battery range:
The current battery range is calculated as the range of the battery multiplied by the battery capacity (in kWh) and the battery percentage.
In this case, 151 km * 57.5 kWh * 100% = 168 km.

Number of charging stops:
The number of charging stops is calculated by dividing the total distance by the current battery range and rounding up to the nearest whole number.
In this case, 336.5 km / 168 km = 2, rounded up to the nearest whole number is 2.

Total time including charging stops:
The total time including charging stops is calculated by adding the trip time with the total time spent on charging.
In this case, 3 hours + (2 * 30 minutes / 60 minutes) = 3 hours + 0.5 hours = 3.5 hours.
